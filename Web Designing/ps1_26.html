Since 1933,The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard to find titles for the discerning reader.Unlike the
bigger bookstore chains,the Bookworm offers:
  • Friendly,knowledgable and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats:Esmeralds,Catherine,Dulcinea and Beatrice

Our hours are 10am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7pm on weekends
